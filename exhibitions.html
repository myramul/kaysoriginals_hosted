Impressionist Horizons: The Art of Claude Monet

May 11, 2025

Exhibition Info

Artist: Claude Monet

This exhibition celebrates the breathtaking landscapes, shimmering water lilies, and atmospheric gardens that define Claude Monet’s artistic legacy. Visitors will journey through his iconic series and explore how Monet’s innovative brushwork and keen observation of nature transformed the art world.

The exhibition opens on May 11, 2025 at Kay's Originals, with an opening night lecture on Monet’s influence and a guided tour of his most renowned works.

Radiant Rhythms: The Art of Alma Thomas

June 8, 2025

Exhibition Info

Artist: Alma Thomas

Celebrating the vibrant and dynamic artistry of Alma Thomas, this exhibition showcases her signature mosaic-like patterns and bold use of color. As a pioneering African American woman in the art world, Thomas broke barriers with her innovative abstract style, inspired by nature, music, and the cosmos.

The exhibition opens on June 8, 2025 at Kay's Originals, with an opening reception featuring a discussion on Thomas’s legacy and a hands-on painting workshop inspired by her techniques.

Dreamscapes: The Surreal World of Salvador Dalí

July 26, 2025

Exhibition Info

Artist: Salvador Dalí

Step into the dreamlike universe of Salvador Dalí, where melting clocks, elongated figures, and bizarre landscapes challenge perceptions of reality. Through paintings, sculptures, and multimedia installations, Dreamscapes offers an immersive journey into Dalí’s surreal visions.

The exhibition opens on July 26, 2025 at Kay's Originals, featuring a surrealist-themed opening event with live performances and interactive installations.
